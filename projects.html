HuzzHub – Brainrot Jia.Seed Hackathon Submission

🏆 Winner of the "This Project is Awesome" Award! 🏆

Welcome to HuzzHub, the ultimate meme-powered AI assistant platform!

HuzzHub is designed to enhance your conversations with humor, charm, and wit. From dropping iconic pickup lines to mastering the art of conversation, our AI assistant—"Chill Guy"—is here to help you level up your rizz. 😏

Features

  • 🔥 Meme-Powered AI: Generate witty and engaging responses based on meme culture.
  • 💬 Pickup Line Mastery: Chill Guy helps you craft the perfect icebreaker.
  • 🤖 Real-Time Interaction: Built using Next.js, Firebase, and Gemini LLM for instant responses.
  • 📱 Responsive UI: Designed with Material UI for a smooth user experience.

Tech Stack

Frontend: Next.js, JavaScript, Material UI

Backend: Firebase, Firestore

AI Model: Gemini LLM

🔗 Live Project: HuzzHub
